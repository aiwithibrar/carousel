## 2. Canva — Most Design Freedom (But Not Really Free for Carousels)

**URL:** canva.com
**Price:** Free tier + Canva Pro ($15/month)
**Signup required:** Yes
**Watermark:** No (on free tier)

Canva is the most well-known design tool and has carousel templates — but it's not designed specifically for social media carousels.

**The reality:** Creating a carousel in Canva means manually building each slide, choosing fonts, picking colors, and aligning elements. There's no "paste text and get slides" feature. It's a blank canvas, not a carousel generator.

**What makes it stand out:**
- Massive template library
- Full design control
- Lots of export formats

**Limitations:**
- Time-consuming — no auto-generation from text
- Many premium templates require Canva Pro
- Not optimized for LinkedIn carousel workflow

**Verdict:** Great for detailed design work, poor for quick carousel generation. ⭐⭐⭐

## 3. Simplified — Good AI Features, But Has a Paywall

**URL:** simplified.com
**Price:** Free plan limited; Pro from $12/month
**Signup required:** Yes
**Watermark:** Yes (on free plan)

Simplified has solid AI-powered carousel generation and a good editor. The free plan exists but adds a watermark to all exports and limits you to 5 downloads per month.

**What makes it stand out:**
- AI text generation built-in
- Good template variety
- Team collaboration features

**Limitations:**
- Watermark on free plan
- Signup required
- Monthly download limits

**Verdict:** Good tool, but the free plan isn't truly free. ⭐⭐⭐

---

## 4. ContentDrips — Best for Bulk Production (Not Free)

**URL:** contentdrips.com
**Price:** From $19/month
**Signup required:** Yes
**Watermark:** Yes (on trial)

ContentDrips is built for agencies and heavy users who need to produce many carousels quickly, including CSV-to-carousel bulk generation. But it's not free.

**What makes it stand out:**
- Bulk CSV import for multiple carousels at once
- Brand kit support
- Good LinkedIn-specific layouts

**Limitations:**
- No free plan
- Expensive for individual creators
- Not suitable for casual use

**Verdict:** Overkill for individuals. Skip unless you're an agency. ⭐⭐

---

## 5. Adobe Express — Good Quality, Requires Account

**URL:** express.adobe.com
**Price:** Free tier + Premium ($9.99/month)
**Signup required:** Yes (Adobe account)
**Watermark:** No (free tier)

Adobe Express has quality carousel templates and a polished interface. The free tier is reasonably generous, but you must create an Adobe account and the auto-generation from text is limited.

**What makes it stand out:**
- High-quality design output
- Good mobile app
- Adobe ecosystem integration

**Limitations:**
- Account required
- No quick text-to-carousel pipeline
- Limited AI automation on free tier

**Verdict:** Good quality, but signing up for Adobe just to make carousels feels like overkill. ⭐⭐⭐
