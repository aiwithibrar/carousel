Frequently Asked Questions

Is CarouselForge really 100% free?

Yes! CarouselForge is completely free to use with no hidden costs, no signup walls, and no feature limitations. All themes, fonts, and export options are available to everyone.

What text formats are supported?

We support 10+ formats including numbered lists (1. 2. 3.), slide markers (Slide 1:), hook/CTA format, Twitter threads (1/ 2/), markdown headings (## Title), bold markers (**Title**), and more.

Can I use it without an internet connection?

Yes! CarouselForge is a Progressive Web App (PWA). Install it on your device to launch it directly from your home screen like a native app.

What platforms can I create carousels for?

We support optimized sizes for Instagram (1080×1080), LinkedIn (1080×1350), Instagram Stories (1080×1920), Twitter/X (1600×900), and Pinterest (1000×1500).

Do you store my data?

No. Everything runs entirely in your browser. We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your content. Your text, images, and settings stay on your device. Read our Privacy Policy for details.

Can I use CarouselForge for commercial purposes?

Absolutely! You're free to use the generated carousels for your business, personal brand, client work, or any commercial purpose. See our Terms of Service.

Write a social media carousel about: [YOUR TOPIC]

Use EXACTLY this format:

[Main title for the carousel]

1. [First point title]
[2-3 sentences explaining this point]

2. [Second point title]
[2-3 sentences explaining this point]

3. [Third point title]
[2-3 sentences explaining this point]

...continue for 5-8 points total.

Rules:
- Start with ONE title line (no numbering)
- Each point: number + period + title on one line
- Body text on the next lines (2-3 sentences)
- Separate each point with a blank line
- No emojis, no bold, no markdown
- Keep each point under 40 words
- End with a final point, no CTA needed
