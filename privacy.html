Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to CarouselForge ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website and carousel generator tool.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

CarouselForge is a client-side tool that runs entirely in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Text or content you paste into the tool
  • Images you upload (avatars, backgrounds)
  • Generated carousel slides
  • Personal information such as names, emails, or passwords

All content processing happens locally on your device. Your data never leaves your browser.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, certain information may be automatically collected through cookies and similar technologies:

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, browser type, device type
  • Log Data: IP address, browser version, referring URLs
  • Cookies: Small data files used for analytics and advertising purposes

3. How We Use Information

Any automatically collected information is used solely to:

  • Improve and optimize our website's performance
  • Understand how users interact with our tool
  • Display relevant advertisements through third-party ad networks
  • Analyze traffic patterns and usage trends

4. Third-Party Advertising

We use Google AdSense and similar third-party advertising networks to display ads on our website. These services may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites.

  • Google's use of the DoubleClick cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • You may opt out of the use of the DoubleClick cookie for interest-based advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • You may opt out of third-party vendor's use of cookies by visiting Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page.

5. Local Storage

CarouselForge uses your browser's localStorage to save drafts and preferences locally on your device. This data:

  • Is stored entirely on your device
  • Is never transmitted to our servers or any third party
  • Can be cleared by you at any time through your browser settings

6. Data Security

Since we do not collect or store your personal content, the risk of data breaches affecting your carousel content is eliminated. For automatically collected data, we implement industry-standard security measures to protect information.

7. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • Object to or restrict certain processing of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please visit our Contact Page.
