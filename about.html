About CarouselForge

CarouselForge is the fastest way to turn your ideas, articles, and social media threads into beautiful, professional carousel slides — completely free. Try it now →

Our Mission

We believe content creation tools should be accessible to everyone, not locked behind expensive subscriptions or complicated software. CarouselForge was built with a simple mission: make professional carousel design effortless and free.

Whether you're a LinkedIn thought leader, Instagram creator, marketing professional, or student — you deserve beautiful slides without the design complexity.

What Makes Us Different

🆓 100% Free, Forever

No premium tiers, no feature locks, no signup walls. Every theme, font, and export option is available to everyone. We sustain the project through non-intrusive advertising.

🔒 Privacy First

Everything runs in your browser. Your text, images, and generated slides never leave your device. Read our Privacy Policy for full details.

📱 Fast & Installable

Built as a Progressive Web App (PWA), CarouselForge can be installed directly to your home screen for quick native-like access on desktop or mobile.

🧠 Intelligently Simple

Our smart text parser automatically detects 10+ content formats. Just paste your text — we'll figure out the rest. No manual slide-by-slide configuration needed.

Who We Built This For

  • Content Creators: Turn your long-form posts into engaging visual carousels
  • Social Media Managers: Create platform-optimized slides in seconds, not hours
  • Marketers: Build professional brand carousels without a design team
  • Educators: Transform lessons and tips into shareable slide decks
  • Anyone with a story: If you can type, you can create beautiful carousels

Our Technology

CarouselForge is built with modern web standards — HTML5, CSS3, and vanilla JavaScript. We use no server-side processing for content generation. The entire tool runs as a lightweight, fast, client-side application with the following features:

  • Smart Text Parser: 10+ format detection algorithms with confidence scoring
  • Auto-Balancing: Automatic slide content optimization for readability
  • High-Quality Export: 2x resolution PNG rendering via html2canvas
  • App Installation: Service Worker-powered PWA for seamless desktop and mobile installation
  • Auto-Save Drafts: LocalStorage-based draft system that persists your work
